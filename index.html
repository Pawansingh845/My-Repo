💬 HR & Interviewer Questions

🧑‍💼 Questions to Interviewer

  • Could you please share some details about the project?
  • May I know who the client is, if it’s okay to share?
  • What is the team size for this project?
  • What tools and technologies are being used in this project?
  • If selected, what will be expected from me in this role?
  • What will be the day-to-day activities in this role?

👩‍💼 Questions to HR

  • What is the notice period in your organization?
  • What are the working hours or shift timings?
  • Is this a full-time permanent position or a contract-to-hire role?
  • Will there be relocation support or reimbursement?
  • What is the breakdown between fixed and variable pay?
  • What kind of medical benefits or health perks are offered?
